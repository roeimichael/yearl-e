About yearl-e

Each day, a random historical year is rolled (deterministic — same year for everyone). You click the globe where you'd want to live. Your score is based on the actual living conditions of that region in that year.

The 5 factors

  • Safety — derived from the Brecke Conflict Catalog. Region loses points for each active war/rebellion that year.
  • Health — era baseline with manual adjustments for known epidemics, famines, sanitation.
  • Economy — Maddison Project 2023 GDP/capita, ranked into a percentile across the year's coverage.
  • Governance — V-Dem v15 Electoral Democracy Index (polyarchy) for years ≥1789; manual research for earlier years.
  • Religious tolerance — manual research of state religion enforcement, persecution, tolerance acts.

Overall score = 0.30 × safety + 0.20 × governance + 0.20 × economy + 0.15 × health + 0.15 × tolerance.

Data sources

  • Maddison Project Database 2023 — GDP per capita, 1 AD–2022
  • Brecke Conflict Catalog — global wars 1400–2000
  • V-Dem v15 — Electoral Democracy Index 1789–present
  • Natural Earth — country polygons (grouped into historical regions)
  • Wikipedia — year article summaries + manual context citations

Known limitations

  • Region borders are modern country shapes grouped into historical entities — not period-accurate.
  • Pre-1789 governance scores fall back to era baselines where no manual research exists.
  • India and Germany are subdivided by bounding box, not true historical boundaries.
  • Currently covers 1500–1815. Other periods coming.
